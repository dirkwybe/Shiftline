New on iOS

Shiftline is a swipe-driven puzzle built for phone screens.

Slide an entire row or column in one move. Blocks glide until they hit a wall or a locked piece. Align every color with its matching socket to seal the board and complete the stage.

Every move is a new layout to solve.

Shiftline mixes minimal rules with deep sequencing. Lock a color into place, then flow the rest around it. Each stage stacks new patterns, tighter walls, and brighter palettes.

Portrait-first. One-hand play. 100 handcrafted stages.

Features

Swipe to shift the board

Each swipe moves a full row or column, turning the grid into a tactile puzzle.

Lock and solve

Match blocks to their sockets to lock them and build your final arrangement.

Hints when you need them

Request a suggestion if you hit a wall. The puzzle never feels out of reach.

Stage progression

10 stages, 100 levels, and a smooth difficulty ramp designed for replay.
