Quick troubleshooting

Game feels stuck

Restart the level from the pause menu and try a different opening swipe.

No sound

Check the in-game Options menu and your iPhone ring/silent switch.

TestFlight build expired

Send a note and we’ll issue an updated invite when the next build is ready.

When you contact support

Include your device model, iOS version, Shiftline version, and a short description of what happened. Screenshots or a short screen recording help a lot.

We typically respond within 2 business days.
